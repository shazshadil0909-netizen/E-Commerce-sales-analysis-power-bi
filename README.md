
E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)
About the Project

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance of an e-commerce business. It provides insights into revenue, profit, customer purchasing patterns, payment methods, product categories, and city-wise sales.

The dashboard allows users to explore the data interactively and identify trends that can support business decisions.

Project Objective

The objectives of this project are to:

Analyze overall sales performance.
Monitor revenue and profit.
Compare sales across different product categories.
Identify top-performing cities.
Analyze customer payment preferences.
Track monthly profit trends.

Key Findings
Sales Revenue

The business generated approximately 2M in total sales revenue.

Total Profit

Overall profit reached approximately 37K.

Average Order Value

The average order value is approximately 875.54.

Credit Card and COD are the most preferred payment methods

Most customers complete their purchases using Credit Card and Cash on Delivery (COD).

Monthly Profit Fluctuates Throughout the Year

Profit is not consistent across months.

Higher profits are observed in January and November.
Lower profitability is observed during May and December.
Indore records the highest sales quantity

Among all cities, Indore has the highest quantity sold, followed by Mumbai and Pune.

Saree is the best-selling sub-category

Sarees contribute the highest sales quantity among all product sub-categories.
